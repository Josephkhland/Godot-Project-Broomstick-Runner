===Running Phase===
Vertical Movement (Up/Down) between four lanes. [Default Control :W for UP and S for Down].
Cast Prepared Spells (Numbers 1,2,3 or 4) || Aim with Mouse and Click to cast Loaded Spell. Right Click to open a popup menu which allows selecting a different action.
Escape: Pause Menu. Inventory -> Use Consumables
		  . Spells -> View Spells Details 
		  . Rest -> Stop at the current point and Start the Resting Phase.
		  . Map -> Shows the World Map, the Spot where you currently are, the Route you are following, and all available Quests.
